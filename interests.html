My Interests

In my spare time, if i'm not learning how to code, I'm most likely painting. I particularly love to paint vast and captivating landscapes in acrylics and watercolours.

Painting reminds me of programming: envisaging something and working step by step, sometimes in layers or sections, to create a wonderous final piece. To see my portfolio of art pieces please click here.

Aside from that, I love to go on long forest walks and to hike. I'm very fortunate to live in Switzerland which has some of the most beautiful scenery in the world and provides me with a real sense of serenity.

I am currently partaking in a B1 German course and I aim to be fluent within the next couple of years.
